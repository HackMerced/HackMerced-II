What is HackMerced?

HackMerced is UC Merced’s second hackathon. A hackathon is a essentially a competition where you are given 36 hours to think, design, build, and present a cool app of your choice! At our the event as soon as you arrive you will be given the resources (food, hardware, swag, and mentorship) that you can take to produce an amazing hack.

Who can participate?

Frequently Asked Questions

Things we can tell you about HackMerced!

What exactly is a Hackathon?

You (the hacker) spend a weekend to build a project (hacks) in teams or individually. There are tons of workshops for beginners to experts, mentors to help you learn, delicious food from local restaurants to fuel you up, and a bunch of energy drinks to power you through. This is where you can bring your ideas to life!

I’m new to hackathons and/or I don’t have experience. Do I have to know how to code to attend?

We have a ton of resources! And there are loads of workshops for beginners to experts as well as mentors to help you get started.

I'm not a CS student, can I join?

Yup, hackathons are for everyone to participate in! No matter the skill level or major. Hackathons are not just programming, but business planning, brand designing and pitching to judges - the coder will thank you if you tagged along!

Is it free? What should I bring?

HackMerced is 100% free, just show up once you have been accepted! Once you get in all you really need is a valid student ID and a laptop. We provide the rest!

What can I build?

Anything. HackMerced gives prizes based on how cool your tool is, not on some pre-set idea for you.

I have no team or ideas!

No problem! HackMerced has some great team-forming activities to help you find teammates, and many hackers work up great ideas while here.

I don't live near Merced, how do I get there?

HackMerced will provide reimbursements to students outside the Merced area. We will try to arrange busses to fellow UC campuses, Stanford, Fresno State, and other central valley colleges.

I'm a High Schooler, how can I participate?

HackMerced is open to High Schoolers, we will be sending you a complementary release form after you apply - you should let your parents sign and present to us before the event!

Foster Innovation in the Central Valley

HackMerced is funded by the gracious donations and sponsorships provided by organizations and people like you. Funds go to food, supplies and awarding young hackers.

What we have planned

HackMerced is UC Merced’s second major collegiate hackathon. On Feburary 10-12, 2017, the HackMerced Team will invite 500 programmers, designers and data scientists from all schools and backgrounds to participate in a 36 hour hackathon.

HackMerced needs your help

Hackathons provide a controlled environment for future jobseekers to test out their skills and creativity. Participating in a hackathon is a great way to connect with programmers, engineers, and designers at their best and worst throughout those 36 hours. HackMerced as a hackathon provides a unique opportunity for organizations to expand their brand to the central valley during the midst of the Winter recruiting season.

Being a sponsor of HackMerced not only introduces your company to 500 students from schools all over the United States, but also provides an environment where you can test out new APIs, launch big ideas for hackers to build for you, interview potential candidates, as well get an easy tool for searching and qualifying recruits for your company.

How we use sponsorship dollars

HackMerced virtues transparency - we believe that we have a responsibility to notify our sponsors on how we use our capital. HackMerced focuses our budget on ensuring our hackers have the best time at our event by providing them food, snacks, drinks, shelter, tools to hack with (hardware, such as power-cords) and shirts.

The next budgeted item is to bring students from outside Merced either through reimbursments to students or hiring buses to connect our sister UCs from UC Berkeley, UCLA, UCI, UCSD, UC Davis, UCSC and UCR.

Finally our last budgeted items include prizes, server hosting and providing hackers with hackathon events, such as mini hacks games and midnight milkshakes or boba

It’s not all about money

HackMerced is a place for big and small - If you’re a small startup or a restaurant wanting to get their name out there, HackMerced would love to work with you as a sponsor in return for mentors, food, or hardware for our students to play with.
